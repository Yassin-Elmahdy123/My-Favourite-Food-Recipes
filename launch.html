Koshary

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of Egyptian Rice
  • 2 cups of black drenched lentils
  • 100-250 grams of spaghette pasta
  • 250 grams of tomato juice
  • 2-3 spoons of tomato paste
  • 4-5 minced cloves of garlic
  • 10ml of vineger
  • lots of salt, pepper, cumin, dried coriander, and chili powder
  • 4 big spoons of Egyptian organic clarified butter or oil
  • 3 big onions
  • 2 medium tomatos and chili peppers
  • 1 cup of shortly cut noodles

Instructions:

  1. Boil water and put the spaghette in it to cook until aldente.
  2. Put 2 spoons of butter or oil in a pot on medium heat with slices of 1 big onion.
  3. Simmer the noodles and rice together in the pot while stirring for 5 minutes.
  4. Add the drenched lentils to the put, stir the ingredients then add 3 cups of warm or boiling water.
  5. Until the mixture of rice and lentils is cooked, get three small pots on medium heat to make the salsa (tomato sauce), Da'a (garlic-vinegar sauce), and Shatta (chilli sauce).
  6. For the Da'a, add 1 spoon of butter or oil, 2-3 minced cloves of garlic, 1 tablespoon of salt, cumin, dried coriander, and black pepper.
  7. When the garlic's color starts turning to brown, add 10ml of vineger, 100ml of water.
  8. Cut some tomatos and chili peppers and put them to the da'a after boiling it for 5 minutes and leaving it to become cooler.
  9. For the Salsa, add 2 spoons of butter or oil, 2-3 minced cloves of garlic, slices of 1/2 an onion, 2 spoons of tomato paste, 1 tablespoon of salt, cumin, dried coriander, and black pepper.
  10. When the garlic's color starts turning to brown, add 10ml of water and 250ml of tomato juice.
  11. leave the slasa to boil for 5 minutes and leave it off the heat to become cooler.
  12. For the Shatta, add 1 spoon of butter or oil, 2 tablespoons of chilli powder.
  13. Stir for 2 minutes and add 4 big spoons of Salsa and 4 big spoons of Da'a.
  14. Cut some tomatos and chili peppers and put them to the Shatta after boiling it for 5 minutes and leaving it to become cooler.
  15. In a frying pan, fry the slices of 1 and half onions after caoting them with flour and starch
  16. Finally, get a dishing plate, add the rice and lentils mixture, the pasta on top of them along with the salsa, da'a, and Shatta.
  17. Don't forget to top the plate with a generous amount of fried onion slices.



Pasta with chicken panne

Ingredients:

  • 250 grams of medium chicken breasts slices
  • 250 grams of spaghette pasta
  • 2-3 minced cloves of garlic
  • 2 table spoons of salt, pepper, dried coriander, and chili powder
  • 100gm of flour and starch
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 spoons of oil or clarified butter
  • 100ml of tomato juice
  • 2 spoons of tomato paste

Instructions:

  1. boil 250 ml of water and cok the pasta in them for 10-15 minutes.
  2. drain the pasta from water and keep it aside.
  3. put 2 spoons of butter or oil on a pot on medium heat.
  4. Add the minced garlic then the tomato paste then the tomato juice.
  5. Add the spices and keep the tomato sauce boiling while making the chicken panne.
  6. For the panne, put the chicken breasts slices in flour then egg then flour while pressing hard in the flour steps.
  7. Deep fry the panne slices for 10-15 minutes depending on the thickness of the slice.
  8. Serve the pasta on a plate with a chilli powder sprinkle and the panne on top.



Burger

Ingredients:

  • 2 Burger Buns (top & bottom)
  • 100-150 grams burger battie
  • 2 slices of tomatoes, lettus, onions, and jalape単os each
  • 2 spoons of clarified butter or oil
  • 5 grams Burger and Ketchup Sauces
  • 1 Cheese Slice
  • 1 egg
  • salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Put the clarified butter or oil on pan on medium heat for 1 minute.
  2. Add the burger battie and leave it for 5 minutes on each side.
  3. Crack an egg beside the burger battie and leave it for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Sprinkle some salt and pepper on the egg.
  5. While the battie and the egg are cooked, Put the Slices of tomatoes, lettus, onions, Ketchup, Burger sauce, and jalape単os on the bread in any order.
  6. Don't forget to add the fried egg on the top of the burger.
  7. serve the burger with french fries and coke.



Crepe

Ingredients:

  • 100-150 grams of Hotdogs slices
  • 2 spoons of clarified butter or oil
  • 50 grams of onion cubes, colored peppers slices, olives slices, and jalape単os slices
  • 1 Big Sag Bread
  • 5 grams Mayonnaise and Ketchup Sauces

Instructions:

  1. Put the clarified butter or oil on pan on medium heat for 1 minute.
  2. cook the onion slices, colored peppers slices, olives slices, and jalape単os slices for 5 minutes.
  3. Add salt, pepper, chilli powder.
  4. Add the hotdog slices with the vegetables to cook with each other for 10 minutes.
  5. Take the hotdog mixture an put it on a Big Sag Bread loaf.
  6. rap it as you like and serve it with fries and extra sauce.
