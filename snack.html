Yogurt with Biscuits

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of Yogurt
  • 1 12-piece Luxe biscuit pack
  • 1 tablespoon of honey

Instructions:

  1. Open the Yogurt cup and the Biscuit pack.
  2. Stir the yogurt and crush the biscuits.
  3. Put the crushed biscuits into the yogurt and stir.
  4. Add 1 tablespoon of honey then stir.



Fruit Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium apple
  • 1 medium banana
  • 1 medium orange
  • 1 medium grapevine
  • 1 medium pomegranate
  • any fruit juice if available

Instructions:

  1. Peel and cut the fruits into cubes.
  2. place these cubes in a big bowl.
  3. pour any fruit juice if available.



Fried Indomie Noodles

Ingredients:

  • 2 Fried Indome Noodles
  • 10 grams of chilli powder
  • 10 grams of minced garlic
  • 10ml of oil

Instructions:

  1. boil 250ml of water
  2. Add the noodles to the pot of water for 5 minutes.
  3. grab a bowl and add the spices packs of the noodles (taste powder & sweet Soya sauce).
  4. Add the chili powder and the minced garlic to the bowl.
  5. Put the oil on medium heat for 3 minutes to become hot.
  6. Pour the hot oil on the mixture of spices and garlic.
  7. Remove the noodles from the water and add them to the bowl, then stir well and serve



Fankoush

Ingredients:

  • 100 grams of Fankoush
  • pench of salt

Instructions:

  1. Fry the Fankoush into a deep frying pan on medium to high heat.
  2. Wait for 1 minute untill the size of it gets bigger.
  3. get the Fankoushout of the oil into a plate with paper towl on it.
  4. sprinkle a pench of salt on it
