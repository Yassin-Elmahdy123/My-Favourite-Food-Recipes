Egg Omelette

Ingredients:

  • 2-3 Eggs
  • 1 medium tomato
  • 1 medium chili pepper
  • 1 big spoon of butter
  • 1 tablespoon of salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl.
  2. Add 1 tablespoon of salt and pepper on the eggs.
  3. Cut the tomato and chili pepper into small cubes.
  4. Add the tomato and chili pepper cubes into the bowl.
  5. Whisk all the components together.
  6. Put on the oven fire with the pan on it.
  7. Add the butter to the pan until it melts.
  8. Add the egg mixture into the pan and leave them to cook for 10 minutes.
  9. Turn off the fire and serve the egg omelette on a plate.



Shakshouka

Ingredients:

  • 2-3 Eggs
  • 2 large tomatoes
  • 1 medium chili pepper
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1-2 big spoons of butter
  • 2 tablespoons of salt, black pepper, and cumin
  • 1 tablespoon of tomato paste

Instructions:

  1. Put the pan on medium to low heat.
  2. Add 1-2 big spoons of butter to the pan to melt them.
  3. Cut the onion into small cubes then add them to the pan and stir them for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Cut the tomatoes and chili pepper into small cubes then add them to the pan and stir them for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add the tomato paste with 1/4 water cup to the pan to make a sauce texture.
  6. Add the 2 tablespoons of salt, black pepper, and cumin.
  7. Finally, Add the eggs and cover the pan for 5-10 minutes.
  8. Turn off the heat and serve the shakshouka with the pan.
  9. serve them with Egyptian baladi bread



Ta'meia

Ingredients:

  • 2-3 Cups of cooked fava beans
  • 1-2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 medium chili pepper
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 medium bunch of dill, parsley, and fresh coriander
  • 1/4 cup of dried coriander
  • 1-2 tablespoons of salt and baking powder
  • a bunch of sesame seeds

Instructions:

  1. Get the blender and blend the fava beans with garlic, chili pepper, onion, dill, parsley, fresh and dried coriander, salt, and baking powder until smooth
  2. Put a deep pan filled with oil on high heat.
  3. Form small patties from the ta'meia dough and sprinkle some sesame seeds on both faces of them.
  4. Deeply fry them in the pan for 5 minutes.
  5. Get them out into a plate that has paper towels to absorb the excess oil from the fried ta'meia
  6. serve them with Egyptian baladi bread



Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients:

  • 2-3 medium potatoes
  • 1 medium chili pepper
  • 2 big spoons of butter
  • 2 tablespoons of salt, black pepper, cumin, and chili

Instructions:

  1. Cut the potatoes into small cubes and the chili pepper into thin slices.
  2. Boil 2-3 cups of water in a pot.
  3. Add the potato cubes to the boiling water for 10-15 minutes with a pinch of salt.
  4. Add 2 big spoons of butter into the bowl along with 2 tablespoons of salt, black pepper, cumin, and chili.
  5. Rinse the potato cubes and add them to the bowl.
  6. mash them with a fork or potato masher then stir them so that the butter and seasonings are mixed well and the potato mixture be smooth.
  7. Serve with Egyptian baladi bread.
